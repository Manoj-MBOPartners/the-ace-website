The Ace Apartment Owners Welfare Association (TAAOWA)

By submitting the form for stall participation, the resident agrees to the following terms and conditions:

Participation Fee

A non-refundable fee of ‚Çπ1000 per stall is applicable and must be paid in advance to confirm the booking.

Electricity Supply

Electricity supply will be arranged by the association if requested. Please mention electrical equipment in the form for safety checks.

Furniture Provided

A maximum of 3 tables and 2 chairs will be provided per stall. Additional furniture, if needed, must be arranged by the stall owner.

Stall Allocation

Stall locations will be finalized by the Association based on available space and overall event layout. No requests for location changes will be entertained.

Cleanliness & Waste Disposal

Stall owners must maintain cleanliness throughout the event. Waste must be disposed of in designated bins. A cleaning penalty may be imposed for non-compliance.

Noise Restrictions

Playing loud music or using sound systems that disturb other stalls or residents is strictly prohibited.

Prohibited Items

No flammable, hazardous, or open flame items are allowed (e.g., gas stoves, fireworks, etc.).

Decoration & Setup

All stall decorations and setup are the responsibility of the stall owner. Setup must be completed within the designated time window shared by the Association.

Setup & Pack-up Timings

Stall setup must be completed 1 hour before event start time, and dismantling must be done within 1 hour after event ends.

Stall Supervision

Each stall must be manned at all times. The Association is not responsible for any theft, damage, or loss of stall materials.

Safety Compliance

All stalls must comply with basic safety guidelines. Extension cords or other equipment used must be in good working condition.

Non-Transferable

Stall participation is strictly non-transferable. Only the resident who registered may run the stall.

Right to Revoke

The Association reserves the right to deny or revoke stall permission in case of rule violations or safety concerns.

Damage to Property

Any damage caused to common areas or property by the stall setup or operation must be borne by the stall owner.

Resident Participation Only

Only residents (owners or tenants) are allowed to put up stalls. Outsiders are not permitted to host stalls unless explicitly permitted by the Association.

Association's Right to Reject or Remove Stall

The Association reserves the right to reject stall applications or ask a stall to be removed at any point if it is found to be violating guidelines, causing disturbance, or deemed inappropriate for the event. Stall owners must comply with such decisions without objection.
